Our Story

The Computer Science Exit Exam Practice Platform was born out of a simple observation: students preparing for their exit exams lacked a comprehensive, centralized resource to practice and master the material. As a computer science student myself, I understood the challenges of reviewing 15 different courses with hundreds of concepts spread across multiple textbooks and notes.

What started as a personal project to organize my own study materials quickly grew into something bigger. I realized that by creating a platform that combines practice questions, detailed explanations, and study materials all in one place, I could help not just myself but thousands of other students preparing for their exit exams.

Today, this platform serves students across multiple universities, helping them build confidence and achieve better results on their exit exams. I'm proud to be part of their academic journey and success stories.

My Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality exam preparation resources that empower computer science students to master their coursework and excel in their exit exams. I believe that every student deserves the tools and support needed to succeed.

My Vision

To become the leading online platform for computer science exit exam preparation in Ethiopia and beyond, continuously expanding resources and improving tools to meet the evolving needs of students.
