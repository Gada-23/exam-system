Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line or contact me via Telegram.

Can I report an error in a question?

Absolutely! I appreciate your help in improving the content. Please use the contact form and select "Report a Bug" as the subject, and include the specific question and what you believe the correction should be.

How can I contribute questions?

I welcome contributions from students and educators. Please contact me with "Content Contribution" in the subject line, and I'll provide guidelines for formatting and submission.

Is this service really free?

Yes! The platform is completely free for all students. I support the site through Google AdSense ads, which allows me to keep all content accessible without any cost to users.
